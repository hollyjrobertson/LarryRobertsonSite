Scripture of the Month

Stressed?

Is stress holding you hostage and keeping you from rest? Do you feel burdens pressing in that seem too heavy to bear? It seems like we are being pulled and pushed into every direction - by work, family responsibilities, social pressure, even by our past and our future. Sometimes we need help from The Lord to help us deal with our everyday stressors.

Overwhelmed?

You know the signs all too well. A pit forms in your stomach. You lose your ability to focus. And your mind begins to wander uncontrollably, fixating on your fears, worries and concerns. You feel like your drowning. Let God be your Life Guard.

Lost?

There may be times in our lives when we feel lost or abandoned, not knowing where we should go in life or how we could ever get out of the mess we are in. It is a difficult feeling to endure and during those times we feel like God is most distant from us. The good news is that God is already searching for you.
